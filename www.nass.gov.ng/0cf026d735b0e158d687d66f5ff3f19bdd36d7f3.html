219.) Hon. NASIRU BABALLE ILA

Honourable Nasiru Baballe Ila, a two- term member of the Federal House of representatives is a rare gem. The 52-year –old, erudite legislator was first elected into the lower arm of the National Assembly on the platform of the Congress for Progressive Change, (CPC) to represent the good people of the Taruni Federal Constituency, Kano in 2011. Between 2011 and 2015, he served in several Standing Committees of the House which included Privatization, Women Affairs, FERMA, Customs, Industry and National Planning among others. Right Honourable Ila as a true Moslem did not betray the trust his constituents reposed on him. He attracted several projects to his constituency and equally did a lot to empower many of them economically and educationally within his first four years in the House of Representatives. His good deeds no doubt was instrumental to his re-election in 2015 under the umbrella of the All Progressive Congress, (APC) following the merger of his party CPC and other political parties to form alliance that edged out the then ruling People’s Democratic Party, (PDP) . On his return to the green Chambers in 2015, he was appointed Chairman, House Committee on House Services which by the grace of God he has piloted creditably well to the admiration of his colleagues because of his uncommon humility. He equally serves on such other Standing Committees like, Defence, Police Affairs, FERMA, ICT and Customs, just to mention but a few. It is also interesting to note that before he ventured into politics in 2011, Right Honourable Nasiru Baballe Ila was an accomplished businessman. He was groomed by his father Alhaji Baballe Ila, a renowned transporter and philanthropist who gave him the opportunity to manage his haulage company way back in 1989. He later set up his own company, Ila Enterprises Limited which gave birth to several other companies both locally and abroad. They include, Noma Oil Mill Limited which is involved in the processing of ground nuts, soya beans and cotton seeds into oil and cake, Barr Head Kids Company, an off-shore company based in Glasgow, Scotland which processes leather skins imported from Nigeria into finished products for export to other parts of Europe, America and Asia, Ila Cotton Ginneries which also processes raw cotton into lint and seeds for exports and Tam –Chem Nigeria Limited involved in the importation of industrial chemicals. His venture and successes in private business were not accidental. He had a good foundation, having graduated with enviable grade from the prestigious West London College, United Kingdom with an Advance Diploma, an equivalent of Higher National Diploma in Business Studies. The leadership qualities he exhibits today in politics did not also come by chance. This is because in far away Egypt between 1983 and 1984, while in high school as a growing child he became the school prefect in Victory College Alexandria. It was a feat only discipline and hard work could have offered him in such a foreign land that is well respected for ancient wisdom. Similarly in 1994 he was elected the Secretary of the well revered Kano Polo club and was known to be one of the youngest individuals to have served the club in that capacity. Right Honourable Nasiru Baballe Ila is a certified member of the Association of National Accountants of Nigeria, (ANAN). He is a man who God has imbued with grace for charity and service to humanity which he does with joy and humility. He is a great listener and gifted with unrivaled interpersonal skills, pleasant disposition at all times and good team spirit which have endeared him to many, both young and old who to came across him. He is a good communicator in oral and written form and always amenable to criticisms and accommodates other people’s views whenever issues of public concerns arise. All these attributes combine to stand him out an exceptional leader. Honourable Ila also finds joy in horse riding, meeting people, rendering assistance to people and engaging in positive discussions with good spirited individuals...
